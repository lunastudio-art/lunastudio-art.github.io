The Droid Project


The Droid Project is a video game being made in EasyRPG and developed by Studio 403.
This game originally was an idea for a video game inspired by the Mother series that was being developed by another game studio. It originally had a lot of references to the Mother series. But the idea did not meet the vision that the director of that game studio wanted to achieve. But they said that I could turn it into my own game. So I looked over the story once more and decided to remove almost all the Mother references and create a new game of my own.

The Droid Project is a game about:
Friends
Acceptance
Depression
Robots
and most importantly... yourself.

"I'm a totally normal kid! Yep! Nothing wrong with me! How do I know?
My father told me!"

Studio 403 has yet to produce a demo version of this software as it is still in a very early development stage. See Luna Studio News for updates on our projects and games.
